Why Depression is a serious matter?

Depression, known as major depressive disorder or clinical depression, is a mood disorder characterized by an enduring sense of sadness and a lack of interest. It impacts your emotions, thoughts, and behaviors, potentially giving rise to various emotional and physical difficulties. Everyday tasks may become challenging, and there are moments when life may seem devoid of meaning.
